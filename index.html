Reshaping Our World: The Impact of COVID-19

Covid-19 has transformed our lives completely remaining as one of the most severe global catastrophes. Various studies were undertaken to deduce the dynamics of Covid's evolution and its proliferation. Lockdowns, among other measures were implemented in a desperate attempt to curb the spread of the virus. Covid's mutations, time, and the vaccine-creating scientists were engaged in a tense three-way conflict. Every element of life, including social, economic, health, and the environment, has been impacted by the pandemic. Below is a brief sketch of COVID-19's initial trajectory
